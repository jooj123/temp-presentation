MARQ Analytics

Phase 2 Re-development

Recap: What does MARQ do?

Allows Mortgage Lenders, Mortgage Investors and Academic researchers assess risk of loan pools.

How?

  • Visualising Risk
  • MARQ score algorithm
  • Comparing loan pools
  • Applying stress tests and scenarios

Phase 2 Purpose

For the replacement of the MARQ production system with a system which meets the needs of Loan RQ's clients, provides scalability, extensibility and maintainability.

This will help support the future growth and business expansion planned by Loan RQ.

Goals

  • Scalability - cater for more users and more data
  • Extensibility - low barrier to innovation
  • Reliability - precision analytics
  • Robust - private data is secure
  • Performance - smooth user experience

New stuff?

  • MARQ Scores calculated on the fly
  • Fresh new UI
  • Fine grained permissioning
  • More risk weight and scenario features
  • Reworked charting
